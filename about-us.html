About us:

Our company is a major procurer of salted and dried Far East (bracken) fern.

We have been harvesting ferns since 2014. Over the years, we have gained considerable experience in the harvesting process and learned the ins and outs of the procurement across the Russian Far Eastern region as well as across Northern, Southern, and Central Russia.

We harvest ferns in the environmentally pristine area near a taiga village which stands by the mountain river Khor in Lazo district in the Khabarovsk region (the Far East of Russia). Collecting and harvesting are carried out in one place, therefore it takes less than 8 hours between fern collection and salting. The short processing time ensures that the fern’s valuable properties will be preserved. Our ferns have a green color and a pistachio aroma, which indicates that we carefully follow the salting procedure. Our ferns preserve their valuable properties and taste after processing and have an appealing appearance

Fern harvesting begins every May or June. The ferns are held in a concentrated salt solution three times. This procedure ensures maximum quality and increases the shelf life of the fern from 1 to 2 years. It is recommended to keep the storage temperature constant, which is not a problem as salted fern can be stored at a temperature range between -30°C and + 30°C. We can procure from 30 to 100 tons of fern annually. We supply salted ferns in 20 kg plastic food containers and smaller packaging. Dry fern is supplied in 3 kg carton boxes.

Far East (bracken) fern is a unique medicinal plant.
